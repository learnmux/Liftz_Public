Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 12, 2026
Your privacy matters to us. Liftz is built to help you train smarter — not to collect or sell your personal data. This policy explains exactly what we collect, why, and how it's protected.
📋

Information We Collect

  • Account information — name and email address provided via Google or Apple Sign In
  • Fitness profile — age, gender, height, weight, fitness goals, and experience level you enter during onboarding
  • Workout data — exercises, sets, reps, weights, and session history you log in the app
  • Readiness inputs — daily energy, soreness, sleep, and mood scores you provide
  • Device information — basic device type and OS version for app compatibility
⚡️

How We Use Your Information

  • Generate personalised workout plans tailored to your goals and readiness
  • Track your progress, personal records, and muscle recovery over time
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Send optional in-app notifications (you can disable these at any time)

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

🔒

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on our servers and protected with industry-standard encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest.

Authentication is handled by Firebase Authentication (Google). We never store your Google or Apple passwords.

🍎

Apple Sign In & HealthKit

If you sign in with Apple, Apple may provide a anonymised relay email address. We use this only for account creation and communication.

Liftz may request access to Apple HealthKit to read activity and health data (steps, workouts). This data is used only within the app to enhance your experience and is never shared with third parties.

👤

Your Rights

  • Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Opt out of any non-essential data collection

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

🧒

Children's Privacy

Liftz is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

🔄

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we'll update the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
