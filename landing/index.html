  • Workout consistency can be used as an eligibility gate, so winners are real active users.

The Journey

From adaptive workouts to Hunt League — and where Liftz is headed next.

Feb 10

The idea

Tried out other fitness apps and kept hitting the same wall: the same routine, week after week, no matter how ready your body actually felt. That gap became the idea for Liftz.

Mar 14

First prototype

Built the first version — readiness algorithm, quick workout generation, and Live Activity support. Rough around the edges, but it worked.

Jun 15

First TestFlight build

Handed it to friends to actually use, gym sessions and all. Their feedback shaped what shipped.

Jul 3

Launched on the App Store

Liftz went live on iOS.

Jul 2026 Rolling out

Hunt League, Hevy import, and referrals

The next Liftz update adds GPS walking quests, monthly league rewards, Hevy history import, share cards, and invite tracking.

Next Planned

First 100 early users

A controlled iOS rollout with founder-led feedback, tighter onboarding, and the first Hunt League cohort.

After iOS beta

Android and deeper social competition

Android support, stronger friend loops, richer Hunt rewards, and more ways to share progress.
